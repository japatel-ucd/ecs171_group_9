Dataset Description

The dataset used to train the model consists of 12810 training samples and 180 test samples. The samples were taken from a large audiobook database. Our dataset derives from the original Kaggle dataset by removing the Spanish samples and reducing the remaining language sets (German and English) down to 25% of the original size. The training dataset contains 12180 training samples and 180 test samples.
Each sample starts out as a FLAC audio file. Data augmentation was performed in the construction of the original dataset to alter the speed, pitch, and noise. The audio files are then converted into spectrograms.
More information about the original dataset inspiration, samples, labelling, and structure can be found on Kaggle.
